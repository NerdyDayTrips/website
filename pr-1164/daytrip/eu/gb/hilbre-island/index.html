Hilbre Island

Created: June 25, 2025 by BruceR
 Show on map     Visit website    Share page Link copied
to clipboard!

Isolated island nature reserve. It has a strange, otherworldly beauty.

Take some binoculars and a packed lunch, check the tide times and the weather. Dress appropriately for an hour’s walk across the sand. Look out for bird life and wave at the seals.
