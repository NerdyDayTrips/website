Camira - Whitsunday Sailing Day Tour

Created: June 28, 2013 (Updated: June 10, 2025) by Madtravelshop
 Show on map     Visit website    Share page Link copied
to clipboard!

Includes: The thrill of high speed sailing through the famous Whitsunday Islands Time to explore the magnificent Whitehaven Beach Swimming and snorkelling at fantastic inner reef sites amongst colourful marine life (conditions permitting) Morning and afternoon tea Delicious barbecue lunch and all refreshments available throughout the day. Courtesy coach transfers from all local area accommodation (Airlie Beach) to Abel Point Marina Included in your cruise fare is a per head fee which goes towards the management& protection of the Reef by the Great Barrier Reef Marine Authority
