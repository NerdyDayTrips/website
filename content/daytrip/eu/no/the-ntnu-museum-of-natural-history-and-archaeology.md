The NTNU University Museum is a Natural and Cultural History museum located at Kalvskinnet in Trondheim. 

The Museum includes the Department of Natural History, the Department of Archaeology and Cultural History and The National Laboratory for Age Determination. Their scientific collections, long-term series, and material from archaeological excavation are often the starting point for research.
